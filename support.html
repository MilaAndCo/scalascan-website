Frequently Asked Questions

Which iPhones support Scala?

Scala requires an iPhone with a LiDAR scanner. This includes iPhone 12 Pro and later Pro models, as well as iPad Pro models with LiDAR (2020 and later). The LiDAR sensor is essential for accurate room scanning.

How accurate are the measurements?

Scala uses Apple's RoomPlan framework with LiDAR data to generate measurements. While results are generally within a few inches of actual dimensions, accuracy depends on room conditions, lighting, and surface materials. We always recommend verifying measurements on-site before making professional decisions.

What can I export from Scala?

Scala supports multiple export formats: PNG and JPG images of your floor plan, PDF measurement reports, full detailed PDF reports with room statistics, and CSV data with your room measurements. Each room scan is exported individually.

Does Scala collect my data?

No. Scala processes everything on your device. We don't collect personal information, track your location, or use analytics. Your scan data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Scala. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. Apple handles all billing and refunds.

Can I scan multiple rooms?

Yes! Each room is scanned and saved individually. You can organize scans under projects with a shared address and project name, and export each room's floor plan and measurements separately.

Where is my scan data stored?

All scan data is stored locally on your device. We don't have servers or cloud storage — your measurements, floor plans, and photos stay on your iPhone. This means your data is completely private and under your control.

Tips for getting the best scan?

For best results: scan slowly and steadily, ensure the room is well-lit, keep doors open during scanning, avoid highly reflective surfaces (like large mirrors), and try to capture all walls by walking the full perimeter of the room. Smaller, simpler rooms tend to produce the most accurate results.

What is the CSV export?

Scala can export room measurement data as a CSV file containing wall dimensions, floor area, ceiling height, and other metrics. This structured data can be imported into spreadsheet tools or used as reference when entering data into claims software like Xactimate.
