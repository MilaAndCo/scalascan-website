How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

No training required. If you can walk through a room, you can use Scala.

01

Scan the Room

Open Scala, tap scan, and slowly walk around the room. LiDAR captures every wall, door, window, and fixture in real time.

02

Review & Edit

See your floor plan instantly. Use the built-in editor to fine-tune details, add photos, and verify measurements before export.

03

Export & Share

Generate professional PDFs, images, or CSV data. Share directly with clients, upload to your claims system, or save for your records.
